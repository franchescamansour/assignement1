A Little About Cuba

Cuba is the place to be, the place to get the best of both worlds. With the combination of the relaxing beach and a fun nightlife, you are sure to be pleased at all times. You will be treated and served by the most humble people who appreciate every little thing, leaving you with a smile on your face.

Fun Things To Do in Cuba

  • Take a tour around the city
  • Go on excursions
  • Swim with the dolphins
  • Rent a Jeep or Vespa
  • Try fresh fruit and seafood
  • Take a taxi ride
  • Go to the disco
  • Click here for more attractions!: Cuban Attractions

Tips for You!

Here are a few little pointers to take into consideration!

  • Bring money to tip and for excursions!
  • Always have sunscreen on!
  • Bring mugs to fill your drinks with on all-inclusive vacations
  • Bring unwanted clothes with you to gift to the workers instead of tips!
  • Keep an extra baithing suit and change of clothes with you
  • Only exchange the money little at a time (You cannot convert back to canadian currency)
